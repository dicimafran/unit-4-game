Crystal Collector

I shall give you a random number at the start of the game.

There are four crystals below. By clicking on a crystal, you are adding a specific amount of points to your total score.

You win the game by matching your total score to the random number, but beware! If your score is above the random number, you lose.

The value of each crystal is hidden from you until you click on it.

Each time the game starts, the game will change the value of each crystal.
